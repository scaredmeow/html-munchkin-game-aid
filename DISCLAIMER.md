## Scope of this game aid

This project (the "Dungeon Tracker") is an **unofficial, fan-made play aid** for the tabletop card game [**_Munchkin_**](http://www.sjgames.com/munchkin/). It does not reproduce the rules of the game, does not include game text, cards, or art from any Steve Jackson Games product, and is not a substitute for owning the game.

It is intended to run in a desktop web browser as a companion screen at the gaming table — comparable to a paper score sheet or a spreadsheet. It is not distributed as a mobile application.

## What this aid does NOT include

In accordance with the SJ Games Online Policy:

- No reproduction of the rules text of _Munchkin_ or any other Steve Jackson Games product.
- No reproduction of card text, card art, charts, tables, or forms from any Steve Jackson Games product.
- No use of Steve Jackson Games logos, the Eye-in-the-Pyramid logo, or other Steve Jackson Games trade dress.
- No imitation of the appearance of any Steve Jackson Games product.

Any in-app text describing classes, races, or game concepts is original wording authored for this aid and is intended as a player-supplied label — players are expected to apply the official rules from their own copy of the game.
